Instructions (read first):

In this portion of the study, you will complete a chess exercise in which your goal is to move a knight from a1 to a8, stopping along the way at every square which is not controlled or occupied by a black pawn. The knight starts at a1 and must visit every square along the first rank from left to right. After reaching h1, the knight must continue upward to h2, then rightward along the second rank (skipping squares which are controlled by pawns). The knight will continue traversing each rank in a zig-zag fashion until it reaches a8.

Your goal is to complete the task as quickly and accurately as possible. The built-in timer will record your performance. A 10-second penalty will be automatically applied for every error (i.e. illegal moves or attempting to move to a square occupied or controlled by a black pawn).
